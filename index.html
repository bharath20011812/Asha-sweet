Our Story

Asha Sweet Center, formerly known as the Malleshwaram Sweet Moat Stall, was founded by the Late Sri Kantha Prasadji Garg in the year 1951. Being a good natured and generous man, he loved to spread joy and happiness wherever he went, which he continues to spread even to this day with Asha Sweet Center spread across the city, Sri Kanthaprasadji's vision was to take his establishment to greater heights, his son Mr. Narendra Kumar Garg joined the business with the same zeal, it was he who renamed the establishment as Asha Sweet Center in the year 1971, and worked hard to learn, prepare and provide sweets of premium taste and quality.
